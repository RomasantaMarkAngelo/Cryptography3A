Introduction

In today's digital age, it is critical to ensure data security when developing applications. As cyber-attacks get more sophisticated, developers must include strong cryptographic algorithms to protect critical information. This exercise will look at a case study that highlights security vulnerabilities in data storage and management during application development, create a security audit strategy, deploy cryptographic methods, and assess their effectiveness.
